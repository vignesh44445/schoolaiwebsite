Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is AI Ready School?
AI Ready School is India's first complete K-12 AI ecosystem — combining a personalised AI learning companion for students, intelligent productivity tools for teachers, hands-on AI innovation labs, and secure on-premise school infrastructure. It is not a single product. It is an entire AI layer built for schools.
How is AI Ready School different from other EdTech platforms?
Most platforms add AI to existing content. We started from a different question: what does it mean to truly learn in an age of AI? Every product we build is rooted in learning science, designed to make students think deeper — not think less. Our philosophy is Human First, AI Next.
Does a teacher need to know AI or coding to use this?
No. Every tool is built for educators, not engineers. Teachers can create lesson plans, assessments, assignments, and student reports using simple, guided workflows — no technical background needed.
Will students just use AI to complete their homework without thinking?
This is exactly the problem we set out to solve. Our student AI companion is designed to ask questions back, surface multiple perspectives, and guide students to their own answers — it will not do the work for them.
